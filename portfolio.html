Flamingo Paintings

Acrylic and spray paint on OSB.
During lockdown I bought 2 of these 3m long wood oriented strand boards (OSB) at Bunnings and had the team there cut them into 3. I started by spray painting the graffiti. It's actually a phrase of lorem ipsum. At first I couldn't think of what I wanted it to say and thought it would be an inside joke to myself and other designers who would notice. The graffiti was more of a texture apposed to something that had any substance because it wasn't the focal point of the painting.
